Q1: Why might we consider using Git?

 Answer: It makes it easier to work on projects in teams and access your files from any device

Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give an Example.

 Answer: Data Science could be applied to statistics. Data Science involves gathering and analyzing data, which directly
 relates to generating probabilities and data charts in statistics.

Q3: What is the difference between Merging and Rebasing?

 Answer: Merging keeps the history of both branches being merged in a new merge commit, while Rebasing changes the history of the
 project by creating new commits for each commit in the original branch, resulting in a linear history.
